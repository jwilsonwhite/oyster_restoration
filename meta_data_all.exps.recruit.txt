Description of data for the field experiments on oyster recruitment (all.exps.recruit.csv)
These data are used by the Rscript Analaysis_Figure_Code_Fig2_and_Fig4	
	
experiment	codes data belong to experimental rounds 1-4 
complexity	describes reshelling treatment in terms of structural complexity with low, moderate, and high levels
reef	numeric identifier of individual reef location
treatment	caging treatment with cage = predator exclosure as only treatment examined 
density	initial density of adult oysters
id	numeric identifier of experimental unit
avg.recruit	average number of recruits per adult oyster shell observed on monthly basis for three months
