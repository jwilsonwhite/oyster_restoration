Description of data for the field experiments on oyster survival (all.exps.surv.csv)
These data are used by R script Analysis_Figure_Code_Fig2_and_Fig4	
	
experiment	codes data as coming from experimental rounds 1-4 
shelled	describes whether oyster reef was shelled (moderate or high volume) or not
complexity	describes reshelling treatment in terms of structural complexity with low, moderate, and high levels
treatment	caging treatment with cage = predator exclosure, cage.control = procedural control treatment, and control = no caging treatment
density	initial density of adult oysters
reef	numeric identifier of individual reef location
id	numeric identifier of experimental unit
nlives	number of living oysters
dead	number of dead oysters
surv	proportional survival of oysters
