Description of data for the field experiments on oyster survival (all.exps.csv)	
	
experiment	codes deata belong to experimental rounds 1-4 
shelled	describes whether oyster reef was shelled (moderate or high volume) or not (ambient)
complexity	"describes reshelling treatment in terms of structural complexit with low, moderate, and high"
treatment	"cageing treatment with cage = predator exclosure, cage.control = procedural control treatment, and control = no caging treatment"
density	initial density of adult oysters
reef	numeric identifier of invididual reef location
id	numeric identifier of experimental unit
nlives	number of living oysters
dead	number of dead oysters
surv	proportional survival of oysters
